How to read this diagram: The main flow is vertical — an idea enters on Friday and works its way down to Monday execution. At each evaluation gate (Tuesday PM and Bi-weekly), ideas can exit left (returned or continued) or right (killed/parked). The dashed line on the left is the MVP fast-track — ideas with a working prototype skip the Idea Pool and Pipeline Review and enter the Incubation Meeting directly.
